RULES
The object of the game is to remember a number and add it to a number that appears later.
  • You will see a numbers, one at a time.
  • Remember the first two numbers
  • When the third number appears, enter the sum of the first and third numbers
  • When the fourth number appears, enter the sum of the second and fourth numbers.
  • This pattern repeats and you will always add the current number to the second previous number.
